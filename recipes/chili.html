Chili Recipe

Description

This recipe is absolutely delicious, because Abbey made it. Mostly comprised of pantry staples, it is easy to throw in the crockpot in the morning for an easy weeknight dinner.
Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cans of Rotel tomatoes
  • 2 Cans plain diced tomatoes
  • 1 Can kidney beans
  • 1 Can great white northern beans
  • 1 Large onion
  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 Can tomato sauce
  • 1 6 oz can of tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Italian seasoning

Toppings

  • Fritos
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Green Onions
