Popcorn

Description

A delicious, salty snack.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked popcorn
  • 2 Tbls Ghee
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cayenne
  • Chili powder
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook popcorn as desired using half of the ghee.
  2. Melt the remaining ghee and toss the popcorn in it.
  3. Sprinkle with seasonings to taste.
